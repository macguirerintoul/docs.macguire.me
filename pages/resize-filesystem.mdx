
1. Increase disk space from Proxmox GUI
2. `sudo fdisk -l`
3. `sudo cfdisk`
4. `pvdisplay`  
5. `sudo pvresize /dev/sda3`  
6. `pvdisplay`  
7. `lvdisplay`  
8. `sudo lvextend -l +100%FREE /dev/ubuntu-vg/ubuntu-lv`  
9. `lvdisplay`  
10. `resize2fs /dev/ubuntu-vg/ubuntu-lv`
11. `fdisk -l`
